PennTrucks

An Android application that connects local Philadelphia food trucks to customers. Users can explore food trucks around the University of Pennsylvania campus, order food, and write reviews.

I created this app with a team (Helen Liu, Austin Luong, Wenting Sun) in my software engineering class and we developed our project using Agile principles.

Check out our user manual.


RoadTrip Nation

In summer 2016, I went on a month-long roadtrip with 2 other college students in a giant green RV.

We traveled across the continental U.S. and interviewed women in STEM to learn about their careers, life paths, and passion for technology. These interviews were filmed and aired in Fall 2017 on PBS and distributed for education. We hope to inspire young students to learn more about STEM and to help bridge the gender gap.

You can watch my travels here: Season 14: A Balanced Equation.
